About the Game

"Guess the Number" is an engaging and entertaining game that challenges players to use their logical deduction skills to guess a randomly generated number within a specified range. It's a classic game that appeals to players of all ages and skill levels.

How to Play

Setup: The game starts by establishing a range of numbers within which the secret number will be generated. For example, the range could be from 1 to 100.

Guessing: The player begins by guessing a number within the specified range. After each guess, the game provides feedback to indicate whether the guessed number is higher or lower than the secret number.

Feedback: Based on the player's guess, the game provides feedback such as "Too high" or "Too low" to help the player narrow down their choices.

Winning: The game continues until the player correctly guesses the secret number. Upon guessing correctly, the player is congratulated for their success, and they have the option to play again.
